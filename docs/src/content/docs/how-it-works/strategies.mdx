# Strategies

Explore the various investment strategies available on the Axal platform.

## Strategy Categories

### Conservative Strategies
- **Stablecoin Yields**: Earn on stablecoin deposits
- **Government Bonds**: Low-risk fixed income
- **Money Market**: Short-term, liquid investments

### Balanced Strategies
- **DeFi Lending**: Earn interest on crypto loans
- **Liquidity Provision**: Provide liquidity to DEXs
- **Staking**: Earn rewards for network participation

### Aggressive Strategies
- **Yield Farming**: Maximize returns through DeFi protocols
- **Leveraged Positions**: Amplified exposure to assets
- **Options Trading**: Advanced derivatives strategies

## Strategy Selection

### Risk Assessment
- **Risk Tolerance**: Conservative, balanced, or aggressive
- **Time Horizon**: Short-term vs long-term goals
- **Liquidity Needs**: How quickly you need access to funds
- **Tax Considerations**: Tax-efficient strategies

### Portfolio Construction
- **Diversification**: Spread risk across multiple strategies
- **Correlation**: Choose strategies with low correlation
- **Rebalancing**: Regular portfolio adjustments
- **Monitoring**: Track performance and risk metrics

## Performance Tracking

- **Historical Returns**: Past performance analysis
- **Risk Metrics**: Volatility, Sharpe ratio, drawdowns
- **Benchmark Comparison**: Compare to market indices
- **Strategy Attribution**: Understand return sources
