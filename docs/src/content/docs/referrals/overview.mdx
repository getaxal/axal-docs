## How It Works

### For New Users: Get a Head Start

Sign up with a referral link and receive a **2% APY boost** for your first three months. This bonus is applied on top of Axal's base APY rates, giving you an enhanced return as you get started on the platform.

### For Referrers: Earn from Every Referral

When you refer someone to Axal, you earn **50% of the fees** from their earnings. Since Axal charges a **15% fee on profits**, this means you receive **7.5% of each person's total earnings**.

## Example Calculation

Let's say you refer a friend who earns **$1,000** in profit:

- Axal fee (15%): **$150**
- Your referral reward (50% of fee): **$75**
- You earn **$75**, which equals **7.5% of your friend's $1,000 earnings**

If your friend consistently earns $1,000 monthly, you would receive $75 every month from that single referral. This is coming from the Axal fee, and not directly from your friendâ€™s earnings.
