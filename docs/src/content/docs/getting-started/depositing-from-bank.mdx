# Depositing from Your Bank

Connect your bank account and deposit funds directly to Axal.

## Bank Integration

We partner with trusted financial institutions to provide secure bank transfers.

## Setup Process

1. Link your bank account
2. Verify your identity
3. Set up transfer limits
4. Make your first deposit

## Supported Banks

We work with major banks including:
- Chase
- Bank of America
- Wells Fargo
- And many regional banks

## Transfer Limits

- Minimum: $10
- Maximum: Varies by account type
- Processing time: 1-3 business days

## Security

All bank connections use bank-level encryption and security protocols.
