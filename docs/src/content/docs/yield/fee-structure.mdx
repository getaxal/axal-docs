## Fee Overview

We maintain a simple and transparent fee structure designed to align our incentives with your success. Axal takes a 15% fee of yield generated through the platform. Axal does not take any up front fees nor does it take any withdrawal fee. Fees are only generated when users generate returns.

Axal reserves the right to update its fee structure as necessary.
