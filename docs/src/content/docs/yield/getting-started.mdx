## Selecting the Right Strategy

When choosing between our Conservative, Balanced, and Aggressive tiers, consider:

- **Investment horizon**: Longer-term investors may benefit from the compounding effects of higher-yield strategies
- **Risk tolerance**: Match your strategy to your comfort level with market volatility
- **Portfolio allocation**: Consider using different tiers for different portions of your portfolio

## Mobile vs Desktop Experience
