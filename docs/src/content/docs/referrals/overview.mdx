# Referrals

Earn rewards by referring friends and family to Axal.

## How Referrals Work

1. **Get Your Link**: Receive a unique referral link
2. **Share**: Send your link to friends and family
3. **Earn**: Both you and your referral earn rewards
4. **Track**: Monitor your referral earnings

## Referral Rewards

### For You (Referrer)
- **Sign-up Bonus**: $10 for each successful referral
- **Ongoing Rewards**: 10% of referral's trading fees for 12 months
- **Tier Bonuses**: Earn more as you reach referral milestones

### For Your Referral
- **Welcome Bonus**: $25 sign-up bonus
- **Fee Discount**: 50% off trading fees for first month
- **Exclusive Access**: Early access to new features

## Referral Tiers

### Bronze Tier (1-5 referrals)
- $10 per referral
- 10% fee sharing

### Silver Tier (6-15 referrals)
- $15 per referral
- 15% fee sharing
- Exclusive events

### Gold Tier (16+ referrals)
- $20 per referral
- 20% fee sharing
- VIP support
- Custom rewards

## Tracking and Analytics

- **Dashboard**: View all your referrals and earnings
- **Real-time Updates**: See earnings as they happen
- **Performance Metrics**: Track conversion rates
- **Payment History**: Complete transaction history
