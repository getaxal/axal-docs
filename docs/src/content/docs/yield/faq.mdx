## Frequently Asked Questions

<div className="faq-container">
  <div className="faq-item">
    <h3>Is yield fixed or variable?</h3>
    <p>Variable. Displayed APYs are rolling 7‑day averages, net of fees.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div className="faq-item">
    <h3>Where can I see proof of reserves?</h3>
    <p>All vault addresses are viewable on‑chain and linked in the Transparency Hub.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div className="faq-item">
    <h3>Are funds ever custodied off‑chain?</h3>
    <p>No. Capital moves only through Privy session keys to and from audited smart contracts from battle‑tested third‑party protocols.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div className="faq-item">
    <h3>Can I lose more than I deposit?</h3>
    <p>No. Positions are non‑recourse; the worst‑case outcome is your balance going to zero.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div className="faq-item">
    <h3>What happens during protocol emergencies?</h3>
    <p>A 3‑of‑5 Emergency Council can pause new deposits and trigger a full unwinding script to return collateral to users.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div className="faq-item">
    <h3>How is APY calculated?</h3>
    <p>APY is calculated using a time-weighted average of returns, including all fees and costs, and annualized to show expected yearly return.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div className="faq-item">
    <h3>What chains are supported?</h3>
    <p>We currently support strategies on Ethereum, Solana, and select L2s with established security and liquidity.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div className="faq-item">
    <h3>Can I use multiple strategies simultaneously?</h3>
    <p>Yes, you can deposit into multiple strategies across different risk tiers to diversify your yield approach.</p>
  </div>
  
  <div className="faq-item">
    <h3>What if I need my funds urgently?</h3>
    <p>All strategies allow immediate withdrawals (subject to a small fee if within 72h of deposit), though underlying protocols may occasionally have their own withdrawal periods.</p>
  </div>
</div>
