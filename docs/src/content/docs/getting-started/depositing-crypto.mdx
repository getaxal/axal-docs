# Depositing Crypto

Learn how to safely deposit cryptocurrency into your Axal account.

## Supported Cryptocurrencies

We support a wide range of cryptocurrencies including:
- Bitcoin (BTC)
- Ethereum (ETH)
- USDC
- USDT
- And many more

## How to Deposit

1. Navigate to your wallet
2. Select "Deposit"
3. Choose your cryptocurrency
4. Copy the deposit address
5. Send funds from your external wallet

## Security Considerations

- Always double-check the deposit address
- Start with small amounts for testing
- Use only supported networks
- Keep your private keys secure

## Processing Times

Deposits are typically processed within:
- 1-3 confirmations for most cryptocurrencies
- Up to 30 minutes for slower networks
