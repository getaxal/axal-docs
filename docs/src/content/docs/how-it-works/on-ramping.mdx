# On Ramping

Understanding how users can convert fiat currency to cryptocurrency on Axal.

## What is On-Ramping?

On-ramping is the process of converting traditional fiat currency (USD, EUR, etc.) into cryptocurrency.

## Our On-Ramp Process

1. **Account Verification**: Complete KYC/AML requirements
2. **Bank Connection**: Link your bank account securely
3. **Purchase**: Buy crypto with fiat currency
4. **Instant Settlement**: Receive crypto immediately

## Supported Payment Methods

- ACH bank transfers
- Wire transfers
- Debit cards
- Credit cards (select regions)

## Fees and Limits

- **Fees**: 0.5% - 2.5% depending on payment method
- **Minimum**: $10
- **Maximum**: $50,000 daily, $500,000 monthly

## Security Features

- Bank-level encryption
- Multi-factor authentication
- Fraud detection systems
- Insurance coverage
