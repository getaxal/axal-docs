## Getting Started with Axal

Getting started with Axal is simple and takes just a few minutes. Follow these steps to begin earning 6-10% APY on your stablecoins:

### Step 1: Sign Up with Email

1. **Visit the Axal Platform**: Navigate to [www.getaxal.com](https://www.getaxal.com)
2. **Enter Your Email**: Use your email address to create your account
3. **Alternative Sign-Up**: You can also sign up using your Google account for faster access
4. **Agree to Terms**: Check the box to agree to our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service

### Step 2: Choose Your Strategy

1. **Review Strategy Options**: Browse through available strategies like "Axal Prime" (6-10% APY)
2. **Compare APY**: See how Axal's yields compare to traditional options:
   - Axal Prime: 6-10% APY
   - U.S. Treasury Bills: 3-5% APY
   - Robinhood Gold: 4% APY
   - U.S. Savings Account: 0.5% APY
3. **Select Your Strategy**: Choose the strategy that best fits your investment goals
4. **Review Details**: See participating protocols, total liquidity, and yield breakdown

### Step 3: Fund Your Account

You have two convenient funding options:

#### Option A: Bank Transfer (Recommended)
1. **Select "Bank"**: Choose the bank transfer option
2. **Enter Amount**: Specify how much you want to deposit
3. **Choose Method**: Select either:
   - **Bank Account**: Direct ACH transfer from your checking account
   - **Credit/Debit Card**: Instant deposit using your card
4. **Complete Transfer**: Powered by Iron and MoonPay for secure processing

#### Option B: Crypto Deposit
1. **Select "Crypto"**: Choose the cryptocurrency option
2. **Scan QR Code**: Use the provided QR code or copy the wallet address
3. **Send USDC**: Transfer USDC tokens to the provided address
4. **Important**: Only send USDC on the Base network

### Step 4: Monitor Your Portfolio

Once funded, you'll have access to your dashboard where you can:

- **View Portfolio Value**: See your total balance and earnings
- **Track Performance**: Monitor your portfolio's growth over time (24H, 1W, 1M, YTD, ALL)
- **Check Earnings**: View your invested amount and earned yield
- **Manage Positions**: See your current positions and strategy allocations
- **Deposit/Withdraw**: Add more funds or withdraw earnings anytime

### What Happens Next?

- **Automatic Optimization**: Axal automatically allocates your funds across the best-yielding protocols
- **Real-Time Updates**: Your dashboard updates with live performance data
- **Compound Earnings**: Your yield is automatically reinvested to maximize returns
- **No Lock-ups**: Withdraw your funds anytime with no penalties

## Mobile vs Desktop Experience

- **Desktop**: Full dashboard experience with advanced analytics and CSV exports
