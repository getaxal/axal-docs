## Glossary

<div className="glossary-table">
  <table>
    <tbody>
      <tr>
        <td className="term">APY</td>
        <td>Annualised Percentage Yield, net of fees.</td>
      </tr>
      <tr>
        <td className="term">LTV</td>
        <td>Loan‑to‑Value ratio, collateral value divided by borrowed amount.</td>
      </tr>
      <tr>
        <td className="term">Flash‑Loan</td>
        <td>A loan that must be repaid within the same block, enabling complex atomic operations.</td>
      </tr>
      <tr>
        <td className="term">Delta‑Neutral</td>
        <td>A position hedged so net price exposure is ~zero, earning only funding or fee yield.</td>
      </tr>
      <tr>
        <td className="term">Impermanent Loss</td>
        <td>The opportunity cost incurred by providing liquidity compared to holding the assets.</td>
      </tr>
      <tr>
        <td className="term">Yield Farming</td>
        <td>The practice of moving assets between protocols to maximize returns from incentives and fees.</td>
      </tr>
      <tr>
        <td className="term">TVL</td>
        <td>Total Value Locked, the total amount of assets deposited in a protocol.</td>
      </tr>
    </tbody>
  </table>
</div>
